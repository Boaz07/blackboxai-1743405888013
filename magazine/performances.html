LIVE PERFORMANCES

Legendary concerts and performances from Chick Corea's career

1972

Montreux Jazz Festival

With Return to Forever

The breakthrough performance that introduced Return to Forever's fusion sound to Europe. Corea's Rhodes playing on "Spain" became legendary.

1982

Duet with Herbie Hancock

World tour

The historic piano duo tour produced the Grammy-winning album "An Evening with Chick Corea and Herbie Hancock." Their improvisational dialogues redefined jazz piano duets.

1996

Blue Note Tokyo

Akoustic Band residency

A celebrated month-long residency showcasing Corea's return to acoustic jazz with John Patitucci and Dave Weckl. The performances were recorded for the live album "Live from Blue Note Tokyo."

2001

Elektric Band Reunion

North American tour

The original Elektric Band members reunited for a tour celebrating their influential fusion work. Corea's synthesizer innovations were prominently featured.

2011

Mozart Festival

Salzburg, Austria

Corea performed his "Piano Concerto No. 1" with the Bavarian Chamber Orchestra, blending classical forms with jazz improvisation in Mozart's birthplace.
